# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 07/03/2026

Welcome to the Booking System. We are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

## 1. Data Controller
The data controller responsible for your personal data is **The Booking System Administrator**. If you have any questions regarding your data, you can contact the system administrator directly through the application.

## 2. What Personal Data Do We Collect?
We strictly follow the principle of **Data Minimization**. We only collect the personal data that is absolutely necessary for the application to function:
* **Account Information:** Email address and a securely hashed password.
* **Age Verification:** Date of birth (to ensure users are over 15 years old).
* **Booking History:** Records of the resources you have reserved and the times of your reservations.

## 3. Purpose and Legal Basis for Processing
We process your personal data for the sole purpose of operating the Booking System. 
* **Legal Basis:** The processing of your data is based on the **performance of a contract** (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR). When you register an account and accept our Terms of Service, we need to process your data to provide you with the booking functionalities. 
* We do **not** use your data for marketing, analytics, or profiling. Therefore, explicit consent for data processing is not required to use this core service.

## 4. Privacy by Design
The Booking System is built upon the **Privacy by Design (PbD)** principle. For example, while the booking calendar is visible to the public to prevent double-booking, the identities of the reservers are strictly hidden from unauthorized users (guests) to protect your privacy.

## 5. Data Retention
We adhere to the **Storage Limitation** principle. We will only retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If your account is deleted by you or by an administrator, your personal data and booking history will be permanently erased from our active database.

## 6. Your Rights as a Data Subject
Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:
* **Right to Access:** You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
* **Right to Rectification:**  You can request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.
* **Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten):** You can request the deletion of your account and personal data.
* **Right to Restriction of Processing:** You can ask us to suspend the processing of your data under certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact the system administrator.
