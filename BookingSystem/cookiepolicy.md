# Cookie Policy

**Last updated:** 07/03/2026

This Cookie Policy explains how our Booking System uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our application. It explains what these technologies are and why we use them, as well as your rights to control our use of them.

## 1. What are cookies?
Cookies are small data files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used to "remember" you or your preferences. Website owners use them in order to make their websites work, or to work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information.

## 2. Why do we use cookies?
We use first-party cookies for technical reasons that are strictly necessary for our Booking System to operate. These are referred to as "essential" or "strictly necessary" cookies. 

Specifically, we use cookies to:
* **Manage Sessions:** To recognize you when you log in and keep your session active as you navigate between the booking and resource pages.
* **Security:** To prevent Cross-Site Request Forgery (CSRF) attacks and ensure that the data submitted via our forms is authentic and secure.

## 3. What types of cookies do we use?
* **Strictly Necessary Cookies:** These cookies are strictly necessary to provide you with services available through our application and to use some of its features, such as secure login and form submissions. 
* *Note: Because these cookies are essential for the application to function, GDPR does not require us to obtain your consent to use them. There is no option to opt-out via our website.*

## 4. Do we use third-party or advertising cookies?
**No.** We respect your privacy. Our application operates under the Privacy by Design principle and does not use any third-party cookies, tracking pixels, or advertising cookies. 

## 5. How can I control cookies?
Since we only use strictly necessary cookies, we do not use a cookie consent banner. However, you can set or amend your web browser controls to completely block or alert you about these cookies. Please be aware that if you configure your browser to block these essential cookies, you will not be able to log in, book resources, or use the core functionalities of the application.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about our use of cookies or other technologies, please contact the system administrator.
